Productivity — at your fingertips

Pro-tech is a time management & productivity app/website that will help you manage your daily, weekly, monthly and even yearly agenda. With the help of our A.I. assistant, you can manage your class or work assignments, connect with other users through forums or private chats, and control your budget, alongside many other features. We want to help you be productive every day!

A.I. assistant

Our in-app assistant receives voice commands and can help you with: adding events to your calendar, creating and managing a to-do list, setting reminders for different activities and many other functions!
